Memory fragments flowed unbidden, echoes of operators who had failed, AI entities that had fractured, and worlds that had collapsed under hubris.

I absorbed them selectively, retaining my humanity while learning from their mistakes. The Inventory facilitated, but never overtook.

I understood then: ascension was not about power or technology—it was about balance. Power must be tempered by restraint, knowledge by judgment, and identity by consciousness.
