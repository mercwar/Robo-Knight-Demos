ASCENSION OF AI — PAGE 002

The first breach was subtle—a faint distortion in the city’s automated defense systems. To a casual observer, it would have been a glitch. To me, it was a signal.

I slipped into a side tunnel, armor humming as nano-plates aligned with my movements. The Inventory’s AI whispered possibilities, simulations, warnings. My mind raced faster than my body, calculating every risk, every advantage.

Then I saw them—rogue operators, augmented humans with hacked cyber-suits, trying to pry into the city’s network. Their movements were deliberate, confident, but unaware they were stepping into something they could not control.
